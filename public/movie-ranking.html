My Favorite Movies of all Time according to Jarvis

Sports Movie

Friday Night Lights

A great sports movie about a Texas High Football team football season in Odessa Texas. The Permian High School football season was filled with ups and downs. Ultimately, the team banded together as brothers and made it to the State Championship. The would end of losing on the last play.

Action Movie

All the transformer Movies

These are great kids movies about cars and trucks that can turn in to machines known as autobots.

American History

The Titanic

This movie is about a luxury ship that sank in 1912 after hitting an iceberg in the Atlantic Ocean. Killing over 1,200 people. The ship was built to be unsinkable. On its first voyage, it sank. Still to this day, many people talk about the horrific tragedy.
